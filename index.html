Frequently asked questions

Is my personal information kept private?

Please be assured that we take data protection seriously, and your information will only be shared with third parties where they abide by applicable data protection legislation. For more information, please read our Privacy Policy in full.

Does Pooja's shrug fulfill custom orders?

At the moment we are unable to accommodate custom orders. However, please let us know here if there is a shoe you would like in a color or material that is currently unavailable. We are always open to expanding our options!

Why did I notice slight differences among Shrugs?

While the utmost care is taken in crafting our shrug to look uniform, each individual shoe is made by hand, which can lead to slight variations in stitching, seams, etc. This adds to the uniqueness of each pair of shrug , which we embrace wholeheartedly and hope you will too! That being said, any flaws you may notice on the surface on the leather itself are a result of natural blemishes in the hides and not manufacturing defects.

How do I become a Pooja's shrug retailer?

If you are a licensed retail store and would like to carry pooja's shrug, please email us at pooja_shrug@gmail.com. We will be happy to provide you with pricing information and help you set up an account.
